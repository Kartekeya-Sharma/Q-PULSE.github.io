What is Q-Pulse?

Q-PULSE is the quantum club of RV University open to students interested in the cutting-edge field of quantum computing.
It provides a unique opportunity for students to come together and learn from one another, participate in hands-on projects, and be at the forefront of this exciting field. Whether you're a newbie or an expert, Q-PULSE offers a dynamic environment to further your knowledge and grow your career in this field.
At Q-PULSE, we will be partenering with companies handling some of the biggest quantum computing operations across the globe and hosting talks with industry icons and leaders. By gaining membership to Q-PULSE, students will be get access to unique benefits.

  • Our members will get access to cutting-edge quantum technology from industry leading companies handling some of the biggest quantum computing operations across the globe.
  • Students will be exposed to the latest quantum computing technologies and the flagship companies in this area.
  • Students will also get a chance to interact with some of the experts in the quantum industry, while also receiving invaluable learning from their experiences.
